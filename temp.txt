Table \ref{tab:GA hyperp} shows the hyperparameters used in GA. GA is time-consuming. Using the above parameters, a full GA run takes about 30 to 40 minutes.

At the beginning, the population is randomly initialized, and the corresponding morphological parameters of each individual are random numbers within a limited range. The limits and parameter types are given in the table \ref{tab:Optimized parameters}. We hope that through population iteration, we can produce individuals with higher rewards. At the same time, the population converges, evolving from random distribution into several specific small populations with high rewards.
